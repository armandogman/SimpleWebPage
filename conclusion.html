Conclusion

The cybersecurity field is one of growth and need - this was the point made by Sydney Lake in their 2022 article “Companies are desperate for cybersecurity workers—more than 700K positions need to be filled” for Fortune Education. This pattern of need is directly reflected in our findings that cybersecurity brings undeniable value to each of the three pillars of sustainability, with continuous growth expected in this context for this dynamic field. Our economic and social systems have become integrally rooted in technologies that require protection, and as the world becomes increasingly digital, even an intuitively analog system like the environment is becoming progressively tied to these systems, all the same.

The thing to do, then, and what is actively being worked on by big tech companies like Microsoft with their cybersecurity skills initiative, is to fill the demand in this segment. There is a need for talent in this profession, and the push for it will leave each of its corresponding pillars of sustainability all the better.
